Mission Statement

I am a junior in the information systems program at Brigham Young University with a passion for financial analysis. In my career, I aim to leverage technology in a financial role in the telecoms industry. To that end, I have accepted an offer from Comcast NBCUniversal to intern in their finance department throughout summer 2017 in Los Angeles, CA. I will apply the technical experience I have garnered in my classes and as a digital media developer for BYU Broadcasting to complete analytical tasks efficiently and discover methods to impact the bottom line.

I am a firm believer that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts and I believe that my technically savvy yet socially conscious point of view will increase the synergy of any team. I have experience accomplishing objectives with individuals from cultures around the globe and believe that my fluency in both spoken and technical languages will be an asset to my employer.
